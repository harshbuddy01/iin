Introduction

Welcome to IIN ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. When you visit our website and use our test series services, you trust us with your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. Please read this carefully as it will help you understand what we do with the information that we collect.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, such as:

  • Name — To personalize your experience
  • Email Address — For communication and account management
  • Phone Number — For important updates and support
  • Payment Information — Processed securely by our payment gateway partners
  • Test Performance Data — To track your progress and provide insights
  • Device Information — To optimize user experience across platforms

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for these purposes:

  • To provide and manage your access to the test series
  • To send you login details and test results via email
  • To process payments via third-party payment gateways
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To communicate important updates and notifications
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To analyze usage patterns and enhance platform performance

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share data with service providers necessary to run our services.

Your information may be shared with:

  • Payment Processors — To handle secure transactions
  • Email Service Providers — To send you important communications
  • Analytics Providers — To understand user behavior and improve services
  • Legal Authorities — When required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure database storage with regular backups
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with industry-standard security practices

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Objection — Object to certain processing of your data
  • Portability — Request transfer of your data
  • Withdraw Consent — Revoke consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.
